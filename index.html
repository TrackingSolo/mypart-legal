MyPart — Choir Assist App

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026 · Contact: ftk.feh@gmail.com

MyPart is a rehearsal and playback assistance app for choir singers. It plays part-specific guide notes (Soprano, Alto, Tenor, Bass) in sync with accompaniment, displays notation and lyrics, and helps you rehearse offline. This policy describes what data the app collects, why, and how to delete it.

Account Sign-In

MyPart requires an account to use cloud sync, playlists, and calibration profiles. You can sign in with:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Facebook Login

Signing in shares your email address and account identifier with MyPart via Firebase Authentication. We use this only to identify your account and keep your data synced across your own devices — it is never shared with other users or sold to third parties.

Data We Store

MyPart uses Cloud Firestore (Google Firebase) to sync content tied to your signed-in account:

  • Custom arrangements and score metadata you import
  • Playlists and setlists
  • Annotations you add to a score
  • Earpiece/accompaniment calibration profiles and session history
  • Subscription tier (Free / Plus / Pro)

This data is private to your account. It is never visible to, or shared with, other users of the app.

Microphone Access

MyPart requests microphone permission to support optional live pitch/timing assistance and Bluetooth latency calibration during a session.

  • Microphone audio is processed live, in memory, on-device only.
  • Raw audio is never recorded, saved to disk, uploaded, or transmitted anywhere.
  • Microphone access is only active while a session or calibration screen is open and the permission is granted. You can revoke it at any time in your device's app settings.

Advertising (Free Tier)

The Free version of MyPart shows ads served through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, AdMob collects a device advertising identifier and basic app-interaction signals, which are shared with Google. This applies only to the Free tier — Plus and Pro subscribers do not see ads.

You can review or reset your advertising identifier, and opt out of personalized ads, in your device's Google Settings → Ads.

In-App Purchases

Plus and Pro subscriptions are sold and processed through Google Play Billing. MyPart does not collect or store your payment details — purchases are handled entirely by Google Play, and your subscription status is synced to your account so it's recognized across your devices.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No audio recordings of any kind
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash-reporting SDK is currently integrated
  • No sale of personal data to third parties

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, Cloud Firestore. See Google's privacy policy for how Firebase infrastructure handles data: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob — ad serving on the Free tier. See Google's ad policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Play Billing — subscription purchases.
  • Facebook Login — optional sign-in method. See Meta's privacy policy: facebook.com/privacy/policy

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your MyPart account and all associated data directly in the app — no need to contact support.

  1. Open MyPart and make sure you're signed in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab (or Settings, from the menu) — both offer the same option.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. A confirmation dialog explains that this permanently deletes your account and everything in it — your arrangements, playlists, annotations, and calibration profiles — and that it cannot be undone. Tap Delete Account again to confirm, or Cancel to back out.
  5. If you haven't signed in recently, you may be asked to sign in again to verify it's really you before the deletion can proceed. Complete the sign-in prompt and the deletion will continue automatically.
  6. Once deletion completes, you're signed out and returned to the sign-in screen. Your data — cloud library, playlists, annotations, and calibration profiles — is permanently removed from our servers at that point.
If you'd rather not use the in-app flow, you can also request deletion by emailing ftk.feh@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

Children's Privacy

MyPart is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Effective date" above will be updated and a notice will be shown in the app.
