MyPart — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

MyPart ("the App") is a choir rehearsal assistance app. This policy describes what data the App collects and how it is used.

Account Data

If you create an account (sign-in), we collect your email address via Firebase Authentication to identify your account and sync your data across devices.

Synced App Data

When signed in, the App stores your library, rehearsal sessions, playlists, and related settings in Firebase Firestore/Storage so they sync across your devices. This data is not shared with third parties and is not sold.

Bluetooth

The App uses Bluetooth solely to measure and compensate for audio playback latency to connected headphones/speakers. No Bluetooth data is transmitted off your device.

No Advertising or Analytics Tracking

The App does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or crash-reporting telemetry.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated synced data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Deletion removes your account record and synced library/session/playlist data from our servers.
